NIT DELHI

Achievement through Brilliance

NIT Delhi, as an institute, strives to provide world class education and an intellectually stimulating environment to its students with a mission to help them develop into individuals with technical and professional competence of the highest degree. Being in the capital of India, NIT Delhi has an edge over the others; in terms of availability of resources, and the best talent of the nation. Within a short span of time we are sure to make a mark in the field of education. Moreover, we have prominent professors, highly experienced and expert faculty. Our mission is to produce human resource with creative and innovative ideas, competitiveness with high intellect and professional ethical values and to impart holistic education along with inculcating high moral values in the students. In addition to this, NIT Delhi has a diverse batch of students with interests across the fields like IT industry, Research, Core engineering , Analytics, Finance and Consulting.

ABOUT NIT DELHI:

Founded in 2010 by the Government of India, NIT Delhi strives to provide a world class education to its intellectually stimulated students. Its underlying mission is to promote the development of individuals who boast world-class technical and professional competence in their respective fields. As a fully funded governmental body, NIT Delhi was established as an institute of national importance during the 11th Five Year Plan under the Ministry of Human Resource Development. Through generating cohorts of motivated students, NIT Delhi aims to continue on its path of achieving international distinction for its commitment to developing technologies for society through the cultivation of knowledge and research.


selection process

Leveraging the advantage of being located in the capital of India, NIT Delhi attracts highly competent minds from all over the country. The cutoffs of the various programmes makes sure that only the best minds make it through the campus. The admissions are conducted on the basis of the highly esteemed entrance exams : B.Tech Programmes : JEE Mains(from 2013), earlier AIEEE conducted by the CBSE SAT (Admissions for Foreign Nationals / Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) under Direct Admission of Students Abroad) M.Tech Programmes: GATE - Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering jointly conducted by IISC and the IITs PhD Programmes: Entrance test and interviews conducted by NIT Delhi.

NIT conclave:

NIT Conclave is the annual gathering of the intellectual cream of the nation for the sessions of unparalleled brainstorming and exchange of ideas that aims to promote interaction among the students of different NITs. In the year 2014, our students won the second prize at the NIT Conclave held at NIT Surathkal. International and National Participations : The institute promotes the participation of the students to represent India at international level . Recently the students have been a part of 100 members delegation organized by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports. Inhouse participation at Rashtrapati Bhavan : To be mentioned with great honor and delight that NIT Delhi was also the host to the first ‘NIT scholars in residence programme at Rashtrapati Bhavan’. Moreover, the students of the college enthralled the President’s Estate with their mesmerizing performances, which included dance, dramatics and music.

Student Life at Campus

NIT Delhi aims at achieving holistic development for each and every student. Apart from being thoroughly involved in their academics, students are also indulged in various extracurricular activities that provides them with immense opportunities to showcase their talent in many sundry fields; from technical to artistic skills. The institute hosts multitude of inter branch events and cultural and technical fest that are solely managed by its students that provides them with work experience crucial for them to excel in their career.

Clubs :

Taking great interest in the overall development of students, the college welcomes the knack of students and encourages them to participate in various inter college and inter branch events providing them with a platform to put forward their hidden abilities. The outstanding performances of the students at various levels is evident from the existing societies : dance, music, dramatics, photography, arts, tech, debating, gaming, quizzing, and workshops formed. All these clubs are run exclusively by the students. Timely meetings of the clubs and active participation of the scholars ensure the proper functioning and the execution of the purpose of their existence.


Social Reformation Cell :

The social reformation cell, established in 2014 is a voluntary organization with the vision to revive the condition of the nearby villages by providing teaching instruction in the primary and secondary schools by the students of the college. It also aims at identifying other social issues relating to health and education of the children and women empowerment, thus contributing to the development of the nation.Recently tree plantation drive and various other competitions were organized on the occasion of ozone day.


Cultural activities:

NIT Delhi offers a holistic lifestyle for the student community. Various clubs and committees are functional in the campus for students with varied interests in fields of literature, technology, culture and sports. Many different events are organized by these clubs during a semester. ‘Kickstarters’ is an inaugural event for every new batch of B.Tech students organized by Alphaz: the Literary and Debating Society of NIT Delhi. It gives students an opportunity to get a taste of college life and interact with one another. In addition to this, we have had many events organized by the Dramatics club, Dance club and the Music society such as Aharya, I love the 90s, and Karaokes, respectively. These exhilarating events have definitely provided the students some respite from their regular academic schedule. Furthermore, NIT Delhi hosted its introductory cultural night, Echoes’14, followed by its cultural festival Saptrang’15. Both events were of immediate thrill and success within the college.


Sports:

The institute hosts and participates in Zeal, the annual sports festival. Students eagerly await the festival as it provides them with the opportunity to showcase their talent in various outdoor and indoor sports. Scholars are always encouraged to participate in the inter-NIT and other National sports tournaments.

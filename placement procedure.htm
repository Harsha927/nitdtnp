Rules and Regulations: Placement Procedure

  1. Placement office sends invitations to companies/organizations along with relevant information
  2. Interested companies show their intent to faculty in-charge TNP, NIT Delhi for recruting through tnp@nitdelhi.ac.in
  3. The companies fill a Job notification form (JNF) for each profile they wish to hire for
  4. Student information may be shared with the company if required
  5. Placement office allots the dates as per the convenience of the company for the conduction of a pre-placement talk/interviews
  6. After the completion of the selection procedure on campus, company is required to announce the final list of the students on the same day itself. If a student is selected, the job is registered against him/her in a particular category and he/she would not be allowed to appear for more interviews in that category as per placement policy

Placement Office / Facilities

The Placement Office is responsible for campus placement at NIT Delhi. The Training and Placement Officer, the Placement Office staff and the student coordinators handle various necessary tasks in the office like inviting companies, scheduling & executing placement related activities and carrying out all official communication with recruiters and with students.
The Placement Office is well-equipped with excellent infrastructure to support every stage of the placement process.
Facilities include:
  1. Seminar halls to conduct workshops and pre-placement talks
  2. Tele-interview rooms
  3. Video-conferencing facility
  4. Fully computerized and air conditioned office
  5. Infrastructure to support online and written screening tests

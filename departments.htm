Computer Science and Engineering:

Computer science is the discipline that studies the structure, function, and applications of computers as well as the interconnection of computers.
Advances in networking and human-computer interfacing technologies have further created a digital culture. The ubiquitous nature of computers in today�s workplace and home environment is making computer literacy a requirement not only for all professionals in industrial societies but also for everyone living in the digital culture.
Covering topics in the areas of foundations of computer science and computer engineering, artificial intelligence, networking, computer graphics, multimedia computing, software and web technologies, and data and knowledge-based systems, the Computer Science programs at this institute are dedicated to educate students and to advance research in computer and information technology; as well as to assist in the growth and development of the information industry in the region.

Electronics and Communication Engineering:

Electronics and Communications Engineering is the study of electronic components, integrated circuits, microprocessors and communication systems. It consists of designing, testing and maintaining the manufacturing of electronic equipment and communication systems. This branch of engineering has a key place in the field of computers, electronics, electrical, communication systems etc.

Electrical and Electronics Engineering:

Electrical and Electronics Engineering is all about power electronic, electrical components, integrated circuits and power systems (power machines). It consists of study, designing, testing and maintaining the manufacturing of electrical equipment, power management and power systems. This branch of engineering plays a vital role in the energy industry, power management etc.

Mechanical Engineering:

Mechanical Engineering is a diverse subject, which involves design, analysis and manufacturing from small machine parts and devices to large systems. We aspire to have a distinguished tradition of excellence in the theme areas ranging from thermal, mechanics, design and manufacturing to CAD/CAM/CAE. Currently department offers the first year courses in B.Tech. Engineering (common for all branches). Undergraduate and Postgraduate programs in mechanical engineering discipline are expected soon.

Applied Sciences:

The Applied Sciences scholars program is an undergraduate curricular program innovatively designed for students to develop interest and showcase their achievements in the natural sciences, mathematics, or social sciences. The program is based on a special general education core that provides an academic base.

Humanities:

The Humanities and Managment Scholars Program is an undergraduate curricular program innovatively designed for students to develop interest and showcase their achievements in the humanities. The program is based on a special general education core that provides an academic base.

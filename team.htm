TEAM

The Placement Office at NIT Delhi is in charge of campus placements for students of the institution. The three main contributors to the Placement Office include the Training and Placement Officer, the Placement Office staff and the student coordinators of our college. From inviting companies to scheduling and executing placement related activities, our Placement Office also carries out all official communication between recruiters and students. Moreover, the office also has to corroborate and ensure that both the recruiters and students feel at ease and comfort while the interview process takes place.
We, at NIT Delhi, place great emphasis on the relationships fostered between our graduating students and recruiting organizations. For this reason, we hope that this program allows for a mesh of new alliances, where our graduates will find themselves in a position to push themselves as well as provide a helpful, creative and fresh mind to the organizations that they will pair with.
Despite being in its nascent stages, NIT Delhi has earned its name and is being counted amongst the best in the country. No wonder it has been drawing the best of the students across the country year after year. This readymade pool of talent is further groomed by the faculty and the institute and converted to engineers with a bright future. The talents this lot of students is effectively channelized. Numerous companies are showing keen interest in hiring the NIT Delhi students and every year the salary is showing an upward trend. Well known companies like Amazon, Housing.com, Balmer Lawrie, Paytm, Samsung, L&T construction and Mitsubishi have visited the campus and hired students.
Our foremost aim is to place the utmost importance in maintaining fruitful liaisons with recruiters and alumni and look forward to make this relationship a successful one.
